ABOUT BASKETBALL

Basketball is a fast-paced team sport played worldwide.It was created in 1891 by Dr James Neismith,where two teams of five players aim to score by shooting a ball through a 10-foot hoop.Played on a rectangular court,the game features dribbling,passing,and intense defence,with points awarded as 2 or 3 per goal.

BASICS

Objective:Score more points than the opposing team by shooting the ball into their hoop.

Teams:2 teams of 5 players each on the court and 7 or more on the bench,with both teams being able to substitute players multiple times.

Gameplay:4 quarters(12 minutes or less in each quarter depending on the leagues rules).

KEY RULES

Scoring:1 point (free throws),2 points(inside 3 point line),3 points(beyond 3 point line)

Ball movement:Dribble or pass;no double dribbling(holding the ball in between dribbles) or travelling(moving more than 2steps after holding the ball).

Fouls:Personal contact(hitting or pushing an opponent);teams get a limited number of fouls per quarter.

LEAGUES

Basketball leagues are competitions where teams play against each other to determine the best team.The top3 best and most popular basketball leagues are,The National Basketball Association (NBA),The Chinese Basketball Association (CBA) and The EuroLeague.

The NBA

The most famous league,based in the US,featuring 30 different teams and popular and famous players such as LeBron James and Stephen Curry.

The CBA

Chinas top league,known for its fast-paced games,it featuring 20 different teams and players such as Zhou Qi and Jeremy Lin

The EuroLeague

A top European league with a total number of 20 teams from various countries like Spain,Greece and Turkey.

POSITIONS

Basketball positions are roles players take on the court.

Point Guard (1)

Ball handler,sets up offense,distributes the ball.Usually smallest player on the team.

Shooting Guard (2)

Scores from outside,creates own shot and is usually the best scorer on the team.Usually bigger than the point guard.

Small Forward (4)

Versatile scorer and defender.Usually bigger in size than the shooting guard and point guard.

Power Forward (4)

Scores in the paint,rebounds and is usually the strongest player on the team.Usually the biggest player on the court.

Center (5)

Dominates the paint,blocks shots and rebounds.Usually the biggest most dominant player on the court.
